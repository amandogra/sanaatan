_Ravan Judh Ajaan Kiyo Tab,
Naag Ki Faans Sabey Sir Daaro.
ShriRagunath Samet Sabey Dal,
Moh Bhayo Yeh Sankat Bhaaro.
Aani Khages Tabey Hanuman Ju,
Bandhan Kaati Sutraas Nivaaro._

> When Ravan attacked by surprise and
put Cobra nooses around everyone's head.
Sri Ram (Raghunath) along with his whole group (monkey army),
felt (they) were in great danger.
By bringing Garud (King of Eagles), O hanuman Ji,
you cut everyone's shackles and freed them.
