_Laal Deh Laali Lase Aru Dhari Laal Langoor
Bajra Deh Daanav Dalan Jay Jay Jay Kapi Soor_


> (The one with) red body, smudged with vermillion, beholder of 'aru', red Colobinae.
(Whose body is) as strong as Vajr (a celestial weapon), (who is) the destroyer of demons,
hail (that) Monkey God!

## Purport
Lord Hanuman is the real problem solver. He has helped many Gods, Demi Gods multiple times. Solving human problems for him is very easy.

In Hindu scriptures, it is advised to pray to him as he will save you from any kind of danger and solve all of your problems.
